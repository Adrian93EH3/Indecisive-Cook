Flip the Coin using the button below!

Never flipped a coin before? You choose heads or tails before you flip, then flip the coin. If the coin lands on the side you chose, you have to cook dinner tonight! Heads is the side with the... well, the head. Tails is the side with the Bald Eagle. Now, get flippin! Don't forget!
Heads means you're eating out tonight, tails means you're cooking.
